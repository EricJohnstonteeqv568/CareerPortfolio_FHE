# CareerPortfolio_FHE

A **confidential career portfolio platform** that allows employees to create encrypted professional profiles containing project experiences, skill certifications, and performance records. Using **Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)**, the system enables secure skill-to-role matching without revealing sensitive employee data, fostering internal and external career opportunities while protecting privacy.

---

## Project Background

Human resources and career management often struggle with balancing employee privacy and effective talent utilization:

- Employees may hesitate to share detailed experiences due to confidentiality concerns  
- Centralized HR systems risk exposing sensitive career data  
- Skill-to-role matching often requires full visibility into employee profiles, creating privacy risks  

**CareerPortfolio_FHE** addresses these challenges by enabling encrypted portfolio creation, storage, and matching. FHE allows HR teams or automated systems to evaluate candidate fit without accessing raw profile data.

---

## Core Vision

The platform empowers both employees and organizations by ensuring:

- Employees retain ownership of their career data  
- Sensitive project experiences and certifications remain encrypted  
- Secure, encrypted skill matching for internal promotions or external recruitment  
- A trustless environment for career growth and talent discovery

## Features

### Portfolio Management

- **Encrypted Profile Creation**: Employees create profiles with project history, skills, and certifications, all encrypted.  
- **Skill Validation**: Certifications and performance records stored securely in ciphertext.  
- **Dynamic Updates**: Employees can update profiles securely while maintaining full privacy.  
- **Portfolio Versioning**: Historical records preserved in encrypted form for audits or performance tracking.  

### Matching & Analytics

- **FHE-Based Skill Matching**: Evaluate candidate fit for roles without decrypting individual profiles.  
- **Role Recommendation**: Generate secure job suggestions based on encrypted skills and experiences.  
- **Internal Mobility Optimization**: Match employees to projects or teams securely and efficiently.  
- **Talent Analytics**: Compute workforce trends and gaps while preserving individual confidentiality.  

### Privacy & Security

- **End-to-End Encryption**: Employee data encrypted from creation to analysis.  
- **Ownership of Keys**: Employees control encryption keys and decide which data to share.  
- **Zero Data Exposure**: HR and managers can match skills without accessing raw profile data.  
- **Immutable Records**: Career profiles cannot be tampered with once submitted.  

---

## Architecture

### Data Layer

- **Encrypted Career Records**: Project history, skills, and certifications stored as ciphertext.  
- **Encrypted Role Metadata**: Job requirements and skill criteria encrypted for secure matching.  

### Computation Layer

- **FHE Engine**: Performs arithmetic and logical operations on encrypted employee profiles.  
- **Matching Module**: Calculates skill fit and role compatibility under encryption.  
- **Analytics Module**: Provides aggregate workforce insights without exposing individual data.  

### Interface Layer

- **Employee Dashboard**: Visualizes secure summaries of profile matches and recommendations.  
- **HR Analytics Interface**: Displays aggregate trends and candidate fit securely.  
- **Recruitment Portal**: Allows encrypted matching for internal and external hiring opportunities.

## Usage Flow

1. **Profile Creation**  
   Employees encrypt their project history, skills, and certifications locally.  

2. **Secure Matching**  
   FHE-enabled algorithms evaluate profile-to-role fit without decrypting sensitive data.  

3. **Decrypted Recommendations**  
   Authorized outputs, such as role suggestions or team placement advice, are revealed securely.  

4. **Continuous Updates**  
   Employees update encrypted profiles as new skills and projects are completed.
